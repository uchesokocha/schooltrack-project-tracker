📌 Note to Client — Infoworks Nigeria

This tracker is a live view of the Schooltrack V3.0 development progress. Every feature listed is drawn directly from the Requirement Document v2.1. Module statuses are updated at the end of each sprint. The development team can update feature statuses by clicking the status dots. Final delivery of MVP 1 is targeted for October 2026, with the system designed to scale to 30 million students and 15 million parents from day one. All data handling is aligned with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA 2023).
